Welcome to Rushton’s chainmail, chainmail has been around for 1500 years and is one of more common armor from the Middle Ages, we invite you to look at our history page if you want to learn more on the history of chainmail.

We invite you to check out the tutorial page you learn how to make chainmail in your backyard, whither it be pot scrubbers, gloves, or armor. There is always something you can make.

We welcome all those with free time on their hands and a want to learn the history and the art of chainmail.

Our purpose is to share and educate others on the history of chainmail and its use in medieval times, and to show others a common way to make it at home. As well as a few items they can make with this method.
